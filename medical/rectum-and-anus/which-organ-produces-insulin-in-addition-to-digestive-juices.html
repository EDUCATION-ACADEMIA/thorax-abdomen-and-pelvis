Which organ produces insulin in addition to digestive juices?
A
mouth
B
gallbladder
C
pancreas
D
liver
Explanation: 

Detailed explanation-1: -Pancreatic hormones include: Insulin. This hormone is made in cells of the pancreas known as beta cells.

Detailed explanation-2: -The beta cells in your pancreas produce insulin. They make up about 75% of the hormone-producing cells in your pancreas. Insulin helps your body use sugar for energy. Glucagon is produced by your alpha cells, which make up about 20 % of the hormone-producing cells in your pancreas.
